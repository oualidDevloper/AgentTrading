We cannot afford to hold a volatile, crashing asset hoping for a macro pivot that might not come soon enough.

That usually led to deeper drawdowns and an inability to buy back in lower because capital was trapped. We need to learn that respecting price action and macro conditions is just as important as the fundamental thesis.
